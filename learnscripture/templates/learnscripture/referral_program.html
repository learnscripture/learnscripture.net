If you like LearnScripture.net and want to spread the word about it, our referral program will help you get discounts on the subscription fees.

What you get

If you get one person to sign up to LearnScripture.net and become a paid member, you get a 10% discount on your subscription fees.

If you get three people to sign up to LearnScripture.net and become paid members, you get a 20% discount on your subscription fees.

The discount will apply if the referrals have become paying members when you next have to pay your subscription fees, and the discount will remain every time after that if the referrals are still paying members.
